To everyone new to the CODING world like me

2018-10-02

The first step is always difficult.

1. where to get started with a new technolygy?

  • see guide doc
  • see tutorial video
  • follow a good textbook
  • read source code of examples, try to catch the key point and basic usage
  • give a practice

No one can be always right, the key is to know whether you are right or wrong.

2. how to evaluate your thought is right or wrong?

  • can you see the output? try your thought and see how output change, and check whether it meet your prediction or not.

when your thought got wrong, you may get confused by something.

3. how to clarify your confusion?

  • search by key words in your situation, because probably somebody has gone through the same and leave a solution.
  • check fully document, and clarify the confusing concepts.
